Class Ext.ux.netbox.date.DateOperator

Extends Ext.ux.netbox.core.Operator.
Defined in DateOperators.js

This is the general class for all DateOperators (with the exception of DatePeriodOperator)
NB: The value of a date operator (ie: value[0].value) will be always in the following format: Y-m-d H:i:s

      String format - The format of the date. The date format should be divided from time format by a space. If you provided only the date format, the time field will not appear.
      Supported formats: 
      Format  Description                                                               Example returned values
------  -----------------------------------------------------------------------   -----------------------
d       Day of the month, 2 digits with leading zeros                             01 to 31
m       Numeric representation of a month, with leading zeros                     01 to 12
Y       A full numeric representation of a year, 4 digits                         Examples: 1999 or 2003
y       A two digit representation of a year                                      Examples: 99 or 03
H       24-hour format of an hour with leading zeros                              00 to 23
i       Minutes, with leading zeros                                               00 to 59
s       Seconds, with leading zeros                                               00 to 59
